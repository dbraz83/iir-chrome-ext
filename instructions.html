Study Instructions

Welcome to the study, you will be given time to
  1. Read the task description;
    • You can access this description at any time throughout the task!
  2. You will then be asked to answer a short questionnaire about this task;
  3. Once this has been complete, click submit and the task begins;
  4. You will have 10 minutes to complete the task;
    • You can end early if you think you are done.
  5. Your aim is try and resolve the scenario by finding online documents (websites, pdf's etc) that you think best help;
  6. Bookmark those documents;
  7. Once the timer reaches 10 minutes or you end the task, complete the post task questionnaire;
  8. Please continue until all 4 tasks are complete.
