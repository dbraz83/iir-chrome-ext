Study Instructions

Welcome to the study.
  1. Read these instructions - which are available at any time during the study;
  2. Before you begin you will be asked some questions about yourself and your IT experience;
  3. You will then begin the study;
  4. Read the task description;
    • You can access the description for the current task at any time throughout the task from the main menu (see #1 above)
  5. You will then be asked to answer a short questionnaire about this task;
  6. Once this has been complete, click submit and the task begins;
  7. You will have 10 minutes to complete the task;
    • You can end early if you think you are done.
  8. Your aim is try and resolve the scenario by finding online documents (websites, pdf's etc) that you think best help;
  9. Bookmark those documents using the browser - if you need help please ask!
    • You can bookmark as many or as few documents as you want.
    • Try and aim for as many as you think are useful and relevant!
  10. Once the timer reaches 10 minutes or you end the task, complete the post task questionnaire;
  11. Please continue until all 4 tasks are complete.
  12. Once all tasks are complete please follow on the onscreen instructions and indicate to the researcher that you are finished. DO NOT CLOSE THE WINDOW OR SHUTDOWN THE PC, Thankyou!
